Our Story

Welcome to Old Tbilisi, where we bring the heart and soul of Georgian cuisine to Brussels. Our restaurant is named after Georgia's capital city, known for its rich history and culinary traditions.

Our family recipes have been passed down through generations, and we take pride in offering authentic Georgian flavors using the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking methods.

From the famous cheese-filled Khachapuri to tender Mtsvadi and delightful Khinkali dumplings, every dish tells a story of Georgia's diverse culinary heritage.

Chef Giorgi Kvinikadze
